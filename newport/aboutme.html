About Me

Hi, I'm Nate Bowers and I love skiing. Ever since I was put on skis at 2 years old, I have spent countless winters in the mountains shredding. I love to ski with my family, especially my brother, who is always outperforming me and drives me to get better. My main focus is big mountain skiing but I am proficient in both park and racing. My home mountain is Squaw Valley in Tahoe. I have also spent significant time at Alpine Meadows(Tahoe), Deer Valley(Utah), Alta(Utah), and Snowmass(Colorado).

My ski rolemodel is Chris Benchetler. He is an amazing backcountry skier and artist. He has his own line of big mountain skis and I regularly use them. Chris can be found driving all over the USA in his custom sprinter van and skiing any chance he gets.
